Column names	Column description
family	The family of the species 
genus	The genus of the species 
table1	Whether or not the species is found in Table 1
species	The species being estimated, where sp. is used for an unknown individual species and spp. used for all the species in a genus
level	Whether the ovule number estimate is based on information about the species, genus, or family
number	Best estimate of the ovule number for the species
Remove	Whether or not the data should be removed. Removal of data is mostly because of duplicate values through citation, but occasionally a value is removed because it appears to be an error (see google sheet for detailed descriptions)
