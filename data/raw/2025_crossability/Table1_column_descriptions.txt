Column Name	Description
family	The family of the species being compared
genus	The genus of the species being compared
species1	The first species in the comparison
species2	The second species in the comparison
duplicate	Indicator for whether the row is a duplicate entry (yes1 = regular duplicate (measured twice);  yes2 = duplicate with two different types of data)
remove	For duplicates which row to remove, keep the better data (based on measure, data completeness, and more recent papers)
PI	Whether the pair is strickly phylogenetically independent (chose pair with best data when there are options; priority = most closely related, best ovule number data, best genetic distance data)
PI_name	Names for PI pairs
intraspecific_value_sp1	Success of species 1 intraspecific crosses
intraspecific_value_sp2	Success of species 2 intraspecific crosses
interspecific_value_sp1_sp2	Success of species 1 (mom) by species 2 (dad) interspecific crosses
interspecific_value_sp2_sp1	Success of species 1 (mom) by species 2 (dad) interspecific crosses
sp1_hybrid_proportion	Proportion of hybrids after mixed pollination of species 1
sp2_hybrid_proportion	Proportion of hybrids after mixed pollination of species 2
RI	Reproductive isolation index between species 1 and species 2
measure	Type of data used to generate RI value (fruit set = measures that don't count the number of seeds; seed set = number of seed produced per pollination (included failed crosses); seed number = number of seeds produced per fruit (failed crosses not counted))
